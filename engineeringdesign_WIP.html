Positive Train Control and Signal Systems

Caltrain

​The Rail Safety Improvement Act of 2008 mandated the use of PTC (positive train control) technology to prevent accidents on US railroads. In September 2019, Caltrain began the Revenue Service Demonstration phase, operating PTC on regular passenger trains, on the San Francisco Peninsula. PTC interoperability work continues with the adjacent Union Pacific, Altamont Corridor Express, and Amtrak railways.

Prototype Low-Cost Motorized Peeler

Trees That Feed Foundation

​As a major crop in Jamaica and Haiti, breadfruit can potentially strengthen food security and en­tre­pre­neurial ventures on the island. ​The Trees That Feed Foundation aims to improve the throughput of breadfruit processing, which involves peeling, shredding, drying, and packaging.


The "Bolt", our motorized prototype peeler, removes the breadfruit's sticky, rough skin in as little as 10 seconds compared to up to a minute with hand-peeling. For material selection and complexity of assembly, local manu­fac­tur­ing and supply costs as well as an alternative manual operation were taken into consideration.

Featured in Northwestern Ideas: Breadfruit Peeler.
