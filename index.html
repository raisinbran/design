Hey there!

I'm a senior in the Manufacturing and Design Engineering (MaDE) program at Northwestern University's McCormick School of Engineering and Applied Science. MaDe is a hybrid of mechanical engineering, industrial engineering, and user-centered product design.

In addition, I am studying toward Northwestern's Institute for Sustainability & Energy Certificate (ISEN) in order to further my interest and gain skills in sustainable design and life-cycle analysis.

Since childhood, I have been fascinated with art and design iteration (e.g. new editions of consumer products); as an engineer, I can shape my creativity within the “engineering rigor” so that my ideas are ready for real-world implementation.

During my time in college, I've also discovered a love for machining and shopwork (that's when I knew I chose the right major), computer-aided design, and product sustainability and cost efficiency.
