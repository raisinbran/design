As a kid I'd bawl when my Lego creations fell apart. Sometimes, though, I concentrated all my attention on rebuilding and improving a creation until it agreed to defy physics - and my peers' creations. There was always something to be improved, and something that needed a wild solution.

With one foot in engineering and the other in art, I can formulate human-centered ideas, knowing that they meet the "engineering rigor" and are ready for real-world implementation.
