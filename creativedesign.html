The Daily Northwestern / 2013 - 2017

​Every publishing day, the ad production artists are responsible for setting up the newspaper's advertisement layout, working in conjunction with the newsroom, and publishing clients' ads in the best form. Observing several inefficiencies in the current process and outdated information in the old training/reference manual, I also revised said manual so that new production artists learn faster, and so that more current knowledge and best practices are retained between hires.
Sample Dummy Ad Layout
Revised Training/Reference Manual (v.4)

Over the course of my work, I noticed that the manual needed significant revisions in several areas. These issues would also be brought up by other production artists. I therefore took it upon myself as a personal project to improve its content and layout. Changes include font standardization, rewritten step-by-step instructions, and media reformatting. For the curious, here is the full revised manual.
